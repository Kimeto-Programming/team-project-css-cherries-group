About Us

Healing with Heart & Science

MediCare General Hospital has been the cornerstone of community healthcare for over two decades — combining medical excellence with genuine human compassion.

ISO 9001 Certified

Founded in 1998, MediCare has grown from a modest clinic to a 400-bed multi-speciality hospital equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic and surgical technology.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality healthcare to every individual with dignity, empathy, and clinical excellence.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative hospital network, setting new benchmarks in patient care and medical research across the region.
